Privacy Policy

AI Field Matcher stores your profile data locally in the browser. It does not transmit your data to the developer or any remote service unless you explicitly choose to link an account (Google People or Microsoft Graph). In that case, after your consent, only selected profile fields are imported and saved locally.

Data We Store Locally

  • Names, email, phone
  • Addresses (Home, Work, Shipping)
  • Optional per-site mapping preferences

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Browsing history or page content
  • SSNs, payment card numbers, or sensitive identifiers

Form Filling

The extension reads labels/attributes of fields on the current page to classify them and fills values from your selected local profile. It does not send page content anywhere.

Security & Compliance

  • Built on Manifest V3; all executable code is packaged.
  • No remotely hosted code is executed by the extension.
  • Any optional OAuth uses the browser's identity flow.
